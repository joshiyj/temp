Top Places in Nagpur :



1. Ambazari Lake


Ambazari Lake is Nagpur's largest lake, known for its serene beauty. Once a water source, it's now a popular spot for picnics and leisurely walks. The adjacent garden, though under renovation, adds to its charm.



2. Futala Lake


Futala Lake is Nagpur's iconic water body, known for its colorful fountains and lush surroundings. Built centuries ago, it's a popular spot for relaxation, boating, and enjoying the evening's light show.



3. Zhilpi Lake


Zilpi Lake is a serene, off-the-beaten-path escape near Nagpur. Surrounded by lush greenery, it offers a peaceful picnic spot away from the city's hustle. While it lacks developed amenities, its natural beauty and tranquility make it a perfect getaway for nature lovers.



5. Swaminarayan Mandir


Swaminarayan Mandir, Nagpur is a magnificent architectural marvel renowned for its intricate carvings and serene atmosphere. This grand temple is a prominent spiritual center, drawing devotees and tourists alike to experience its divine aura and explore its stunning craftsmanship.
